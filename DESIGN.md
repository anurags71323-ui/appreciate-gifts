# Design Brief

## Tone & Purpose
Premium gifting boutique — heartfelt, refined, warm, modern. Emotional connection through elegant design and generous white space.

## Differentiation
Warm metallics (gold/amber), generous rounded corners, refined serif display typography, soft elevation shadows, cream-and-sand palette.

## Structural Zones
| Zone | Background | Border | Depth | Detail |
|------|-----------|--------|-------|--------|
| Header | Card (warm white) | Subtle bottom border | Subtle shadow | Minimal logo, nav |
| Hero | Background (warm cream) | None | Flat | Serif headline, accent line underline |
| Product Cards | Card (warm white) | Subtle border | Elevation shadow | Gold accent on hover, rounded 16px |
| Footer | Secondary (muted sand) | Top border | Flat | Warm text, legal links |

## Shape Language
- Border radius: 16px (lg), 14px (md), 10px (sm)
- Buttons: 12px rounded
- Cards: 16px rounded
- Spacing: 4px unit, 24px gutter

## Motion & Interaction
- Smooth transitions: 300ms cubic-bezier(0.4, 0, 0.2, 1)
- Card hover: 8px elevation shadow, slight upward shift
- Button hover: gold accent brightens, text weight increases
- No bouncy animations — refined, minimal motion

## Constraints
- Primary color used sparingly on CTAs, accents, active states
- No animations on content sections — only interactive elements
- Minimum contrast ratio AA+ on all text over backgrounds
- White space breathing room around hero, product sections
- Typography hierarchy via size and serif/sans pairing, not color

## Signature Detail
Elegant serif accent line (2px gold) under hero headline — subtle, distinctive, premium.
